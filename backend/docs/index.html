Software Engineering Lab 2 - 2021-2022: Team 2

Introduction

This repo is the osoc selection solution team 2 made for Open Knowledge Belgium. In order to create a selection tool, we created an online platform where coaches can assign students to projects.
The tool is made with a variety of technologies, the main ones being PostGres, Prsima.js, Next.js and Express.js.
An extensive list of all the documentations can be found here.
